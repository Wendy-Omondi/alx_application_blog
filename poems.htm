Voice the Silence

Author Akwin

Share

Voice your choice
It is simple, you can
If it's your own, then you've won
In this day and time, in the way of dime
It's too petty to care most about the material

Voice your choice
However, you can,
It may be a just a fact in your gut
expressed as a mark in your work of art
It may be a part in your act
or just you, as God says you are.

Voice your Choice
To whoever you can
To those that need plain explanation
To the professional whose fulfilment is occasional
To the society that has created deities and ignored Him
To those who only know who they are expected to be
and sadly not who they really are.

Voice your choice
Don't keep it inside
To change a life of strife to one in Christ
From one who survives to one who thrives.
